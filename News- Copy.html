Faculty News

5 January 2023

In the interest of Al-Nahda University to discover the talents of its students in many fields, highlighting them, refining them, and caring for them, students of the Faculty of Computer Science participated in the international competition presented by Huawei ICT Academy for the year 2022-2023, which is a global competition that is held worldwide in the fields of communications and Technology. Our students passed the competition in its three stages, which included: The registration stage, followed by preparation for the exam in one of several tracks: Network Track – Cloud Track – Computing Track The stage of determining the level of each student through the Assessment Exam, based on which 500 students from the first universities are selected for the test stage at the level of the Republic. The stage of dividing students into teams and escalating a team from each track to compete at the level of North Africa Exam, through which it is possible to travel to China and represent Egypt in the global competition.

1 January 2023

Within the framework of joint cooperation between Al-Nahda University and the Supreme Council of Universities, Prof. Dr. Hossam Al-Mallahi – President of the University, and Prof. Dr. Mustafa Refaat – Acting Secretary of the Supreme Council of Universities signed, on Thursday, January 5, 2023, a cooperation protocol to activate training and tests for programs eligible to obtain the “Fundamentals of Digital Transformation” certificate approved by the Supreme Council of Universities, inside Learning Resources Center (LRC) at Al-Nahda University.This was in the presence of Prof. Dr. Mohamed Ali Saleh – Vice President of Al-Nahda University for institutional Development and International Cooperation and The President of Learning Resources Center LRC, and Prof. Dr. Magdi Abdel Qader – Vice President of Al-Nahda University for Education and Student Affairs, and Dr. Ashour Omari – Director of the Electronic and Knowledge Services Center at the Supreme Council of Universities.

16 January 2023

The Faculty of Computer Science organizes a training course in Web ProgrammingUnder the auspices of Professor Dr. Hossam Al-Malahi – President of NUB Dr. Mohammed Ali Saleh, Vice President of the University for Strategic Planning and International Cooperation and Dean of the Faculty of Computer ScienceThe Faculty of Computer Science organizes a training course in web programming as part of the College’s plan to develop the practical skills of students of the Faculty of Computer Science during the summer semester to improve the level of labor market requirements, and the training dealt with a number of important topics in this field such as HTML (intro), CSS (intro), PHP, SQL (MySQL) and WordPress (as application), with a 70% discount in the value of the course for faculty students and graduates.

13 December 2022

On Tuesday, December 13, 2022, Al-Nahda University organized for its students a field trip to Children’s Cancer Hospital 57357. Students of the Faculties of Pharmacy, Computer Science and Business Administration participated in this fieldtrip, who contributed to providing moral support and drawing a smile on children’s faces, as the students participated in many recreational activities, as well as Distributing gifts and toys to children to bring happiness to their hearts. The Public Relations Officer accompanied the students on a tour of the hospital corridors and gave them an overview of the hospital and clinics, the mechanism of work in them, and the importance of these fieldtrips in improving the psychological condition of patients. At the end of the fieldtrip, the students were keen to make a financial donation in favor of the hospital. This field trip coincided with the initiatives and invitations launched by many public figures and artists to urge them to fieldtrip the hospital, donate for children to help them complete their medical treatment, and help the hospital keep its doors open.
